Psalm 15, KJV

**Upload date 8/1/17 **Download an MP3 of this track here.

Good morning Heritage Church, and friends! Thanks for joining us in memorizing Psalm 15. Make sure to say thanks also to Gabrielle, Camille, Lauren, and Caroline for their recording of the Beethoven String Quartet #2 in G maj, Opus 18, Adagio cantibile. That’s Gabrielle and Camille on violin, Lauren on viola, and Caroline on the cello. Also to Scott Young for the great voiceover.

Okay, this link here is to the page you’ll visit every day this month. If you’re using a phone or tablet, you may want to copy-paste the address of this page directly onto your home screen. It will create an icon, like an app, which will take you directly to the site with no fussing around every day to find your way.

Instructions: Simple - gather the family and listen about 3 times in a row every day. That’s it. Try to find a time where things are fairly calm, when everyone is settled down and able to listen with relative attention. The goal is not to be able to recite the Psalm this week - it’s to absorb the text through familiarity with the music, like soaking in a nice, hot bathtub.

Tomorrow, do it again. Easy, right? You’ll hear from me again in about a week.

Have a great day. Jonathon
