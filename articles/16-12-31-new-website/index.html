New web site !

Here we go, 2017…

So I’m excited. It’s been a solid 10 years since I’ve built a site from scratch, and it’s great to see how the tech has changed. After a lot of knocking about, I’ve settled on a great static site engine from the good folks at Hugo. It’s simple enough that I can actually wrap my mind around most of it, and also simple enough that adding content should be pretty painless. Big shout out for Hugo – I would recommend it for anyone looking for a fast-rendering, lightweight engine for blogs or simpler websites.

Looking forward to getting to know you all, and thanks for stopping by. Jonathon
